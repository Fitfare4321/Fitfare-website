Partner cancellations & settlements

Effective date: 18 July 2026 · FitFare Partner

1. Booking cancellations

Consumer cancellation windows are set by FitFare policies and shown to users at checkout. Partner-initiated cancellations of confirmed sessions without adequate notice may reduce payout eligibility and affect partner standing.

2. Payout adjustments

Settlements may be adjusted for refunds, chargebacks, unverified check-ins, fraud, or policy breaches before or after a payout cycle.

3. Disputes

Contact collaboration@fitfare.in with booking id and date. We review within a reasonable business period.

4. Account closure

After offboarding, pending lawful payouts are reconciled. Request data deletion at collaboration@fitfare.in.
