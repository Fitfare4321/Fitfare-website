FitFare Partner Privacy Policy

Effective date: 18 July 2026 · Last updated: 18 July 2026
App: FitFare Partner · Operator: FitFare (“we”, “us”)

This Policy explains how we collect and use personal and business data when you use the FitFare Partner app to onboard, manage listings, verify KYC, receive push alerts, and receive payouts. It is intended to meet Google Play disclosure expectations and applicable Indian law, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP) where it applies.

1. Data we collect

  • Account: email, phone, password/OTP authentication (Firebase Authentication).
  • Identity / KYC: owner name; government ID type and images (Aadhaar / Passport / Driving Licence); PAN; business registration documents (GST, Shops & Establishment, Udyam, incorporation, LLP, partnership deed as applicable); cancelled cheque / passbook images (optional).
  • Bank & payout: account holder name, account number, IFSC; bank verification status. Account numbers are encrypted at rest. We may initiate a ₹1 micro-deposit (“penny drop”) via RazorpayX to validate the account.
  • Business profile: centre/gym details, schedules, pricing, amenities you provide.
  • Operations data: bookings, QR check-ins, attendance, payout statements, support messages.
  • Push notifications: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) device tokens so we can send transactional alerts (for example new bookings). Tokens are stored on our servers and removed when you log out or unregister the device. We do not sell device tokens.
  • Device media: camera and photo library access only when you capture or upload KYC documents, or when you choose to save your centre QR image to Photos.
  • Device & diagnostics: app version and basic device / crash diagnostics needed for reliability and security.

2. Why we use this data

  • Verify partner identity and business legitimacy (fraud & compliance). Partners must be 18+.
  • Enable listings, bookings, and QR attendance
  • Validate bank accounts and pay settlements
  • Send operational push notifications you enable
  • Comply with tax, AML, and payment-partner requirements
  • Provide support and improve the Partner product

3. Sharing (processors)

  • Firebase (Google): authentication and Cloud Messaging (push)
  • Supabase: database and document/file storage under our control
  • Cloud API hosting (e.g. Render): application servers that process Partner API traffic
  • Razorpay / RazorpayX: bank account validation (penny drop) and payouts
  • Reviewers / admins: FitFare operations staff reviewing KYC
  • Law enforcement when legally required

We do not sell partner personal data. We do not use your KYC or bank data for third-party advertising.

4. Sensitive documents & financial data

KYC images, government IDs, and bank details are used only for verification, payouts, fraud prevention, and legal compliance. Access is restricted to authorised systems and staff. After offboarding, we delete or anonymize verification files subject to legal retention needs (for example tax or dispute periods).

5. Retention & deletion

Data is kept while your partner account is active and thereafter as required for settlements, audits, and law. You can request account / data deletion from the Partner app Profile screen or by emailing collaboration@fitfare.in from your registered email. Some records may be retained where legally required.

6. Security

HTTPS in transit, encrypted storage for sensitive bank fields, role-based access for admin review. No system is perfectly secure.

7. Your rights (including DPDP)

Subject to law, you may request access, correction, erasure, and withdraw consent where processing is consent-based (for example optional notifications). You may update profile information in-app where available, re-upload rejected KYC docs per product rules, disable camera / notification permission in device settings, and contact us for a grievance or deletion request at collaboration@fitfare.in.

8. Children

The Partner app is for business users aged 18+ only. We do not knowingly onboard minors.

9. Permissions (Android / iOS)

  • Internet — required to use the service
  • Camera / Photos — KYC document capture/upload and optional Save QR
  • Notifications — transactional booking and operational alerts (optional; you can disable)

We do not request location, SMS, contacts, or microphone access in FitFare Partner.
